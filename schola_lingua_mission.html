Schola Lingua- Mission

"To teach, use, preserve, and practice language diversity"

"To foster multilingualism"

"To enrich technical educational development through language diversity"

Who We Are

Schola Lingua is a private educational collective of speakers of one or more of the target langauges in our programs of study.
Our educators teach target langauges through another, technical education, including agriculture, animal husbandry, the building arts, the fine arts, and practical skills.
Experiential immersion is our strategy. An uninterrupted four years of study at Schola Lingua results in fluency in the target langauge.
